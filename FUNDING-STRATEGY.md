## 1. Analyzed Models

The project investigates the viability of different resource sources for maintaining decentralized technological infrastructure:

### A. Crowdfunding/Sponsorship
Analysis of platforms like GitHub Sponsors and Open Collective for server costs and testing hardware.
*Status: In feasibility study.*

### B. Grants and Research Funding
Mapping of institutions that fund privacy and security research (e.g., technology foundations, academic grants).
*Objective: Financial support for laboratory material.*

### C. Voluntary Donations
Decentralized mechanisms (e.g., Lightning Network) for spontaneous support from the scientific and technological community, without product reciprocity.
